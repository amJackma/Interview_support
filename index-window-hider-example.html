How to use:

  1. Click "Refresh Windows List" to see all open windows
  2. Click "Hide from Screenshot" to hide a window from screen captures
  3. Click "Show in Screenshot" to restore a hidden window
  4. You can still interact with hidden windows normally!

💡 Tip: Use this during screen sharing (Zoom, Teams, Discord, etc.) to hide sensitive windows from your audience.
